Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to Bocor’s FAQ section. Here, we address some of the common questions our clients have about our web design services. If you have any other questions, please feel free to contact us.

Why should I choose Bocor for my web design needs?

We combine creativity and technical expertise to deliver visually stunning, responsive, and user-friendly websites. Our commitment to quality, customer satisfaction, and ongoing support sets us apart from the competition.

How does the design process work?

Our design process typically includes the following steps: Initial consultation to understand your needs and goals. Proposal and agreement on the project scope and pricing. Design phase, where we create wireframes and mockups. Development phase, where we build the website using Bootstrap. Testing and revisions to ensure everything works perfectly. Final delivery and launch of the website. Ongoing support and maintenance as needed.

How long does it take to build a website?

The timeline for building a website depends on the complexity and scope of the project. A basic website can take 4-6 weeks, while more complex projects, such as e-commerce sites or custom web applications, may take several months.

How much does a website cost?

Our pricing varies based on the package and services you choose. Our packages start at $1,500 for a basic website and go up to $10,000+ for fully customized enterprise solutions. We also offer additional services at an hourly rate of $100.

Do you offer custom packages?

Yes, we can create custom packages tailored to your specific needs and budget. Contact us to discuss your requirements and we’ll provide a customized proposal.
